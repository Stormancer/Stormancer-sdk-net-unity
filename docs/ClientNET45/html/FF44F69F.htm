UNRELIABLE0 The packet may be lost, or arrive out of order. There are no guarantees whatsoever.
UNRELIABLE_SEQUENCED1 The packets arrive in order, but may be lost. If a packet arrives out of order, it is discarded. The last packet may also never arrive.
RELIABLE2 The packets always reach destination, but may do so out of order.
RELIABLE_ORDERED3 The packets always reach destination and in order.
RELIABLE_SEQUENCED4 The packets arrive at destination in order. If a packet arrive out of order, it is ignored. That mean that packets may disappear, but the last one always reach destination.
